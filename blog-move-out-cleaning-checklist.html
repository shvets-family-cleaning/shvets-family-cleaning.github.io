Move-Out Cleaning Checklist for Georgia Renters

8 min read November 2024

Moving out of a rental in Georgia? Your security deposit could be $1,000-$3,000 or more. Don't lose it to cleaning deductions. This comprehensive checklist covers everything landlords look for during move-out inspections.

Georgia Security Deposit Laws

Under Georgia law (O.C.G.A. § 44-7-34), landlords must return your deposit within 30 days. They can deduct for:

  • Damage beyond normal wear and tear
  • Unpaid rent or fees
  • Cleaning costs to restore the property

Pro tip: Request a pre-move-out inspection to identify issues before your final walkthrough.

Complete Move-Out Cleaning Checklist

Kitchen Checklist

  • ☐ Clean inside oven (remove all grease and burnt residue)
  • ☐ Clean inside microwave
  • ☐ Clean inside refrigerator (remove all food, wipe all shelves)
  • ☐ Clean freezer (defrost if needed)
  • ☐ Degrease stovetop and drip pans
  • ☐ Clean range hood and filter
  • ☐ Wipe all cabinet exteriors
  • ☐ Clean inside all cabinets and drawers
  • ☐ Scrub sink and polish faucet
  • ☐ Clean dishwasher interior
  • ☐ Clean countertops and backsplash
  • ☐ Mop floor (including under appliances)

Bathroom Checklist

  • ☐ Scrub toilet inside and out (including base)
  • ☐ Clean shower/tub (remove all soap scum)
  • ☐ Remove mold/mildew from grout
  • ☐ Clean shower door tracks
  • ☐ Polish mirrors
  • ☐ Clean vanity inside and out
  • ☐ Scrub sink and polish fixtures
  • ☐ Clean exhaust fan
  • ☐ Wipe light fixtures
  • ☐ Mop floor (including behind toilet)

All Rooms Checklist

  • ☐ Dust and wipe all light fixtures
  • ☐ Clean ceiling fans
  • ☐ Wipe all light switches and outlets
  • ☐ Clean all door handles
  • ☐ Wipe down doors (both sides)
  • ☐ Clean baseboards
  • ☐ Clean window sills and tracks
  • ☐ Clean blinds
  • ☐ Vacuum carpets (consider professional cleaning)
  • ☐ Mop hard floors
  • ☐ Clean inside all closets
  • ☐ Remove all nails/hooks, fill holes
  • ☐ Touch up paint if needed

Commonly Missed Areas

These are the spots landlords always check:

  • Inside oven and refrigerator
  • Behind toilet
  • Shower door tracks
  • Under kitchen sink
  • Garage floor (if applicable)
  • Patio/balcony
  • Air vents and returns

Tips for a Successful Move-Out

  1. Start early: Begin cleaning 2-3 days before your final walkthrough
  2. Document everything: Take photos of every room after cleaning
  3. Be present at inspection: Address any concerns on the spot
  4. Get it in writing: Request written confirmation of property condition
  5. Keep receipts: If you hire professionals, keep the invoice
