Airbnb Turnover Best Practices for 5-Star Reviews

7 min read November 2024

Cleanliness is the #1 factor in Airbnb reviews. A single "dirty" review can tank your bookings. Here's how professional hosts maintain 5-star cleanliness ratings with efficient turnovers.

The 5-Star Airbnb Turnover Checklist

Kitchen (15-20 minutes)

  • ☐ Remove all food and check expiration dates
  • ☐ Clean inside refrigerator
  • ☐ Clean inside microwave and oven
  • ☐ Wipe all counters and backsplash
  • ☐ Clean and sanitize sink
  • ☐ Run empty dishwasher with cleaning tablet
  • ☐ Restock: dish soap, sponge, paper towels
  • ☐ Arrange dishes and cookware neatly

Bathrooms (15-20 minutes each)

  • ☐ Scrub toilet inside and out
  • ☐ Clean shower/tub (check for hair!)
  • ☐ Polish mirrors and fixtures
  • ☐ Replace towels (fold hotel-style)
  • ☐ Restock: toilet paper (2+ rolls visible), soap, shampoo
  • ☐ Empty trash and replace liner
  • ☐ Check for mold/mildew

Bedrooms (10-15 minutes each)

  • ☐ Strip and remake beds with fresh linens
  • ☐ Check mattress protector
  • ☐ Dust all surfaces
  • ☐ Empty and wipe nightstand drawers
  • ☐ Check closet (empty hangers ready)
  • ☐ Vacuum under bed
  • ☐ Check for previous guest items

Living Areas (10-15 minutes)

  • ☐ Vacuum/mop all floors
  • ☐ Fluff and arrange pillows
  • ☐ Dust surfaces and TV
  • ☐ Check remote batteries
  • ☐ Fold blankets attractively
  • ☐ Empty trash cans
  • ☐ Set thermostat to comfortable temp

The Details That Get 5-Star Reviews

These small touches separate 4-star from 5-star:

  1. Fresh scent: Light, clean smell (not heavy air freshener)
  2. Perfect bed presentation: Crisp sheets, hospital corners, decorative pillows
  3. Bathroom luxury: Rolled towels, travel toiletries arranged nicely
  4. Kitchen staging: Clean dish towel on oven, coffee setup ready
  5. Welcome touch: Small snack, handwritten note, or local guide

Common Turnover Mistakes to Avoid

  • ❌ Hair in bathroom: Check drains, floor, and towels carefully
  • ❌ Stained linens: Inspect every sheet and towel before making beds
  • ❌ Dusty surfaces: Guests run fingers along surfaces
  • ❌ Dirty remotes: Wipe down all electronics
  • ❌ Empty supplies: Always overstock toilet paper and toiletries
  • ❌ Previous guest items: Check every drawer and cabinet

Time Management for Turnovers

For a 2-bedroom rental, efficient turnover takes:

  • DIY cleaning: 2-3 hours
  • Professional service: 1.5-2 hours

Pro tip: Schedule checkout at 11am and check-in at 4pm minimum. This gives time for proper cleaning plus buffer for any issues.

Deep Clean Schedule for Airbnb

In addition to turnover cleaning:

  • Monthly: Deep clean mattresses, clean inside oven and fridge
  • Quarterly: Full deep cleaning service, check HVAC filters
  • Annually: Professional carpet/upholstery cleaning, repaint high-traffic walls
