How Much Does Deep Cleaning Cost in Alpharetta?

5 min read November 2024 SHVETS PRO Team

Deep cleaning in Alpharetta, GA typically costs between $219 and $450, depending on your home's size and condition. This comprehensive guide breaks down exactly what affects pricing and how to get the best value.

What's Included in Deep Cleaning?

Unlike standard cleaning, deep cleaning covers areas that don't get attention during regular maintenance:

  • Kitchen: Inside oven, microwave, refrigerator exterior, degreasing stovetop and range hood, inside cabinets (exterior)
  • Bathrooms: Scrubbing tile grout, removing soap scum and limescale, cleaning exhaust fans, behind toilets
  • Living Areas: Cleaning baseboards, door frames, light switches, ceiling fans, window sills
  • Bedrooms: Under beds, inside closets (organizing visible items), dusting blinds

Factors That Affect Deep Cleaning Cost

1. Home Size (Square Footage)

Larger homes require more time and supplies. A 4,000 sq ft home will cost significantly more than a 1,500 sq ft apartment.

2. Current Condition

If it's been over 6 months since your last deep clean, expect to pay 10-20% more due to accumulated buildup.

3. Add-On Services

Common add-ons and their costs:

  • Inside refrigerator: +$35
  • Inside oven: +$35
  • Interior windows: +$50
  • Garage cleaning: +$75-150

4. Frequency Discounts

Signing up for recurring service reduces your deep cleaning cost:

  • Weekly service: 20% off
  • Bi-weekly service: 15% off
  • Monthly service: 10% off

Deep Cleaning vs Standard Cleaning Cost

Standard cleaning typically costs 30-40% less than deep cleaning. For a 3-bedroom home:

  • Standard Cleaning: $174
  • Deep Cleaning: $264

Most homeowners get deep cleaning 2-4 times per year, with standard cleaning in between.

How to Save on Deep Cleaning

  1. Use our first-time discount: Code FIRST15 saves 15%
  2. Book recurring service: Save 10-20% on every clean
  3. Declutter before the cleaner arrives: Less time moving items = lower cost
  4. Bundle with neighbors: Ask about multi-home discounts
